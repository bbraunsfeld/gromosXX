Dihedral Restraints

DIHRESSPEC
# i  j  k  l   delta    phi   w0
  1  2  3  4    90.0  120.0  1.0
END

where i, j, k and l specify the dihedral angle, phi the restraint angle value and w0 is a weight factor (multiplied by the force constant specified in the input file). delta specifies where the maximum of the harmonic potential will be. The restraint is periodic from delta to delta + 360.
