Dihedral Restraints

DIHRESSPEC
# i  j  k  l   delta    phi   w0
  1  2  3  4    90.0  120.0  1.0
END

where i, j, k and l specify the dihedral angle, phi the restraint angle value and w0 is a weight factor (multiplied by the force constant specified in the input file). delta specifies where the maximum of the harmonic potential will be. The restraint is periodic from delta to delta + 360.

Perturbed Dihedral Restraints

PERTDIHRESSPEC
# i  j  k  l  m    n    delta  A_phi  A_w0  B_phi  B_w0
  1  2  3  4  2.0  2.0   90.0  120.0   1.0  160.0   1.0
END

With m and n the exponents of the prefactor l^n*(1-l)^m, A_phi and A_w0 restraint angle and weight factor in state A, and B_phi and B_w0 the corresponding quantities in state B.
